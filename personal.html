My Autobiography

As I grew older, I attended UDM and pursued my passions in BSIT major in Data Science. Along the way, I faced many challenges and obstacles, but I persevered and learned valuable lessons about resilience and determination.

Some of the highlights of my life include I work part time in Divoria. I also experienced setbacks and disappointments, but these moments helped me grow and learn from my mistakes.
