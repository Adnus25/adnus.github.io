Bornday

I was born on 25 at October 2001 in Manila. It was a Rainy day.

My parents were overjoyed to welcome me into the world, and I was the first child in our family. They named me John Joseph , and I have been known by that name ever since.

As I grew up, my parents would often tell me stories about the day I was born and how happy they were to have me in their lives. It is a special day that holds a lot of meaning for me and my family.

Looking back on that day, it is amazing to think about all the experiences and opportunities that have come my way since then. I am grateful for the life I have been given and the people who have been a part of it.
